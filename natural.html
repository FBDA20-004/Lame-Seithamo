Wadi Ghalilah, Duba

Wadi Ghalilah is an area of natural beauty recognised for its dam and its mountain trails. One such mountain climb is famously known as ‘Stairway to Heaven.’ The stairway in this regard represents an old shepherd mountain route and series of stone walkways ascending somewhere approaching 2,000 metres to the summit of Jebal Jais.

Al Qudra Lakes, Dubai

Manmade Palm Jumeirah spans 600 hectares of land and is made from all-natural materials. It took 10.5 million dump trucks worth of sand to create Palm Jumeirah which equates to 94 million cubic metres of sand.

Big Red, Dubai..

Big Red is the largest sand dune close to the city, making it the ultimate spot for desert sports and scenic views. Located near Al Badayer, this dune is particularly busy on the weekends for dune bashing, quad biking and sand skiing.

Jumeirah beach

Jumeira Beach is a white sand beach that is located and named after the Jumeirah district of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, on the coast of the Persian Gulf
