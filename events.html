National Day Celebrations
Commemorating the merger of seven emirate states into one nation, which was founded on December 2nd 1971. National Day is a two day public holiday and a big event in the calendar for locals, who decorate buildings with the UAE flag and colours. Celebrations are marked by events throughout the Emirates and visitors can enjoy concerts, dance shows, car rallies and lots of discounts and free giveaways
