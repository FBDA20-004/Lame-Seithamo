LIFESTYLE IN DUBAI

REAL ESTATE AND FASHION

Dubai has beaches which are very interesting places to visit for example the Kite beach, Black Palace and Royal Island Beach Club. It also have a man-made island that has a 7 star hotel built on top of it. Most people believe that it is a 7 star hotel because it contains many floors compared to any other hotel and also for the way it was built and its look. The city also have other prestige hotel that they rent out to businesses and individuals. Dubai is popular for its tallest building which is said to be the tallest in the whole world and its said to have a height of 829.8 m and its construction started in 2004 and it was officially opened in 2010. The building was named Burj Khalifa and it was built to gain international recognition for Dubai and also to diversify the oil-based economy. Dubai has townhouses, villas and other luxurious apartments which it rents out to individuals and businesses. The city of Gold mostly revolved around traditional Arab and Islamic culture therefore this influence the sense of fashion in Dubai as women wear a long black rope with a scarf that covers the whole body while men wear a dishdasha.
